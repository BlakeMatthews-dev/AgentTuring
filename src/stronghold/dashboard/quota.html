🪙 Coin Currency

Stronghold normalizes model costs into a universal coin currency. Copper is the free daily faucet — use it or lose it. Exchange copper to silver at a reduced rate to save it. Your coin denomination controls model access: silver can only buy silver-tier models and below. Frontier models require gold or higher (purchased).

Denominations & Exchange Rates

Copper buys small/fast models, silver buys capable all-rounders, gold buys frontier reasoning. Ratios: 10 copper = 1 silver, 10 silver = 1 gold, 5 gold = 1 platinum, 2 platinum = 1 diamond.
